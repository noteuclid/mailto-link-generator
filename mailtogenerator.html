'mailto:' Link Generator

A mailto: link is a link that is associated with your default email app. Clicking on a link like this in a browser causes your browser to open your default email app with some values already filled out. These apps are common in email apps and some websites. Click for an example.

On this page, you can generate your own mailto: link. In each box, type out what you want the default email values to be, then click 'Generate'. You must at least specify the default recipient. None of these values are ever stored.
